Mosaic pieces

JULY 9, 2023

This week we have been spending a lot of time working on these beautiful mosaic pieces. Each piece of the mosaic is put together by hand and is painted with many different coats to really give it a stunning look.

Along with the cross pictured below, we will be making a sunset similar to others we have made in the past. For this sunset, we have spent a lot of thought about what is a good way to paint the different pieces. We ended up going with a more realistic fade from warmer colors near the bottom to cooler blues up near the top. The focus of this piece is more on the sunset rather than having all the foreground elements. We think this really narrows down the purpose of the product and what it's trying to say.
