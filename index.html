About us

howesDecor. is a company which specializes in selling handcrafted, upcycled, and refinished furniture and home decor. Our company is local to the Okaloosa, FL. area and sells these products at our booth, #228, located at Defrance Antiques on Beal, Fort Walton Beach, Florida. We created howesDecor. to express our creativity through what we love doing, and have done for over a decade as a hobby. Now we are blessed with an opportunity to turn our passion into profit.
